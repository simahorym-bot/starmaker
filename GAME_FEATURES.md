## üíæ Game Mechanics

### Economy System
- **Starting Money**: $10,000
- **Income Sources**:
  - Music streaming earnings
  - Merchandise sales
  - Tour revenue
- **Expenses**:
  - Recording costs
  - Media appearances
  - Studio upgrades
  - Luxury purchases
  - Energy restoration

### Energy System
- **Maximum Energy**: 100
- **Energy Costs**:
  - Creating single: 10
  - Creating EP: 25
  - Creating album: 50
  - Tour performances: 20
  - Media events: 15
- **Recovery**: Pay $5,000 to fully restore

### Progression System
- **Experience**: Gain from songs, tours, and media
- **Reputation**: Build through media and performances
- **Prestige**: Accumulate through luxury purchases
- **Level**: Increases with experience
- **Fan Growth**: Organic and through activities

### Fanbase Tiers
- **Hardcore Fans**: Most loyal, gained through quality
- **Casual Fans**: Majority, gained through activity
- **Haters**: Can develop based on actions
